A Higher Place

Conceptual Origin

As we looked through our old internet archives/presence for this project, I particularly felt really nostalgic for these little minigames we used to play and that used to be my only destination on the internet for a few years as a kid. I can say that my avatar world is sort of inspired by that, but it is a combination of different web experiences I’ve had throughout my life. I wanted to create a web fun and safe space where monster-slimey looking avatars ruled, so this is kind of what this is.. :)

Process

This project first started out by looking through our mental memory box looking for memories from our internet existence. I came across many things, but I ultimately ended up creating something quite weird. I first ventured into the world of old internet games, old websites i frequented,etc… and started to categorize them. We then moved on and started giving our avatars attributes, characteristics,etc.. Through these brainstorming sessions, I came up wit the idea of creating a virtual adult summer camp where different avatars can meet. The special thing about this world is that each avatar can create their own “life-trailer” so that other avatars can get to know them by just watching one video

Steps

  • Look through all my internet archives
  • Categorize everything
  • Create a perosnality for my avatar
  • Start sketching out my avatars
  • Find a location for my little friends
  • Create a simple storyboard of how the game would work (dynamics)
  • Create stop-motion ish first draft
  • Yay! Final amateur-looking lil trailer is created for my utopian adult summer camp.

Reflection

Despite the confusion and the vague nature of this project, I actually really enjoyed doing this. The assignments I usually got are quite structured and planned out, and the uncertainty of this project first scared me but I actually really enjoyed the creative liberty I got. I also really enjoyed getting to know my classmates a bit through this fun lens.
